# Ditebogo

[English](CREDITS.md) · [简体中文](CREDITS_zh.md) · **Setswana**

Porojeke e ke pheto (fork) ya porojeke ya go bulega ya **Windows 12 Online**
(Windows 12 网页版). Thadiso ya ntlha le bontsi jwa khoutu ya porojeke e di
dirilwe ke batlhami ba ntlha le baagi ba ba lokolotsweng fa tlase. Pheto e e
diretswe **maikaelelo a thuto**, mme e tswelela ka tiro ya bone — tebogo yotlhe
ya motheo e ya go bone.

## Porojeke ya ntlha

- **Leina:** Windows 12 Online (Windows 12 网页版)
- **Polokelo ya ntlha:** <https://github.com/win12-online/win12> *(ga e sa tlhole e bonagala phatlalatsa ka nako ya go kwala mo)*
- **Polokelo ya diphetolelo:** <https://github.com/win12-online/win12-locales>
- **Dilwana tsa setlhogo sa ntlha:** <https://github.com/tjy-gitnub/win12-theme>
- **Webosaete ya porojeke:** <https://win12.tech>

> Go fitlha bogare jwa 2026, polokelo ya kwa godimo le webosaete ya yona di
> bonala di timilwe kgotsa di dirilwe sephiri. Se se umakiwa kwantle ga
> ngongorego epe — re leboga gore tiro e e ne ya abelanwa phatlalatsa, mme re
> batla fela go boloka tebogo ka tshwanelo. Fa motlhami mongwe wa ntlha a batla
> gore tebogo e e fetolwe, kgotsa a kopa gore pheto e e tlosiwe, re tla e tlotla.
